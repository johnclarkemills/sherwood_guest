Welcome to Sherwood

Welcome to our off the grid refuge in the foothills of the Sonoma redwoods. We are fortunate to be the newest stewards of this parcel of the old Sweeetwater Ranch. We look at this privilege as an honor as well as a responsibility. We ask that you do the same.

Covid-19

Out of respect for our quaranteam, please take the following seriously and let us know where you stand before your arrival:

1. If you’ve been properly quarantining for at least 3 weeks, wearing gloves and masks in the supermarket, then you can join us for dinners in the house main house and hang out inside.

2. If you have NOT been safe enough, living in a community house, or generally feel unsafe exposing the rest of us please plan dinners on your own back at the yurt.  You’re welcome to join for Happy Hour and for work days outside but other than that, please keep your distance otherwise.

Either way, upon your arrival please abide by the following:

  • Wash your hands
  • Clean any food items that you are bringing in
  • After unpacking foods please leave the cardboard outside
  • Once you find your room, please feel free to change your clothes and shower

House Rules

  • No fires. Period
  • Be respectful of others and the land during your stay
  • Do not flush anything other than toilet paper! We are 100% off the grid here
  • Take your shoes off when you’re in any of the buildings
    • During the rainy season or when dirty, please use mud room for entry

WiFi & Cellular

The WiFi name is `guest` with no password.

  • Verizon and Sprint work flawlessly on almost all corners of the property. AT&T does not
  • Our WiFi is extremely limited up here. Please do your best to disconnect
  • Zoom and other conference calls will work quite well
  • If you're in the main house, we have TV and Movies you can watch here: video.sherwood.co

Linens & Laundry

We have all your bedding and linen needs covered. The laundry room is located in the main house behind the kitchen.

  • If you're staying in the yurt, please leave your bedding at the main house before leaving
  • Please leave the washing machine door open after using to prevent mold.
  • Detergent is underneath the washing machine
  • If someones laundry is still wet in the dryer please dry it for them

Yurt

The yurt is about a 1/4 mile from the main house by foot. There is a path where can walk across the creek. Driving however you will need to take a ranch road (dirt) across a shallow creek if you want to drive. Although we've taken sedans up there for fun its not recommended. A small SUV or Subaru will have no problem getting out there.

What the yurt has:

  • Fireplace
  • Shower
  • Composting Toilet
  • Stove
  • Refrigerator (3 ft)
  • Cooking utensils
  • Plates, bowls, & cups
  • King size bed & pillows

What you need to bring:

  • Food
  • Bath Towels
  • Warm clothes
  • Flashlight

Checkout

  • Please strip your bed and return the sheets to the laundry room in the main house
  • For those staying in the yurt
    • You are responsible for cleaning and tidying up the yurt for the next guest.
    • You will be introduced to the folks who stayed there before you and the folks who are staying after you.
    • Please empty the composting toilet bucket in the 'Shit Hole' that is designated on the map
  • On your way out, please ask if you can take any trash or recycling down the hill to the trash bins at the corner of Sweetwater Springs and Westside Road
  • Come again and drive safe!
